About Spiral Out Games

Spiral Out Games is currently my failed attempt at a gaming network and social hub.

Being friends with mostly all gamers, I had wanted to create a gaming network that we could all be a part of and be the hub of our social circle. The name was inspired by the song "Lateralus" by Tool.

If Im completely honest, the reason why this is in my Portfolio is that its a placeholder. I didnt design the forum, its a custom-made MyBB theme that I tweaked. I did the logo and imagework, though.
